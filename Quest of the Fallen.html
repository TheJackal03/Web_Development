VR Quest of the Fallen Lite

❮ ❯

Quest of the Fallen is a medieval shooting game with RPG elements. It is one of the first games to combine RPG Fantasy elements with VR gameplay.

You will battle endless hordes of enemies based on DnD mythology in a virtual reality setting. Survive in the intense wilderness, filled with beast-infested forests, horrifyingly infected graveyards, and ultimately reach the gates of hell itself!

You are humanity's last hope as you embark on a legendary journey against the powerful forces of the underworld, led by the fearsome Fallen Angel. Experience fast-paced shooting action and tackle truly challenging levels. Play this VR shooting game as a warrior, magician, or ranger, each utilizing different weapons and abilities.

VR Quest of the Fallen is a brand new addition to the genre of VR shooting games, offering incredible mixed virtual reality gameplay and high-definition textures. Dive into the world of virtual reality and play through a series of progressively difficult levels. Each level will empower your hero, enhancing their abilities. Only the strongest of heroes can challenge the Fallen Angel himself.

Prepare for the ultimate challenge against the forces of darkness and unleash all your skills in the face of the extreme battles that lie ahead. Prove yourself as a legendary hero, and the heavens themselves will reward you. Embrace the breathtaking battles and become the hero of the Holy Hills in no time! VR Quest of the Fallen Lite is a three-phase demo version of the VR Quest of the Fallen game.

    Game Features:
  • Compatible with all VR headsets.
  • Realistic 3D graphics with dynamic lighting effects.
  • Choose between three different heroes with unique statistics and abilities.
  • Engage in thrilling missions set in various environments.
  • Fight corrupted humans in forests, undead in graveyards, and demons in the underworld.
  • Different target points yield different damage outcomes (headshot, body shot, leg shot).
  • High-quality sound effects with realistic battle sounds.
  • RPG elements, leveling system, and straightforward gameplay.
    Gameplay:
  • Insert your device into the VR headset and use gaze navigation and selection.
  • Use the headset or the in-game button to shoot.
  • No need to reload, simply gaze and shoot to kill!
