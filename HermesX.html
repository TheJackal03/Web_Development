HermesX

❮ ❯

In the year 2322, humanity has achieved the ability to travel at light speed, venturing through the vast expanse of the universe in search of new civilizations. Spaceships are deployed to distant planets for exploration.

One such spacecraft is the "HermesX," a cutting-edge vessel with a specific mission to discover new alien life. As a military expert responsible for the security and well-being of the crew, you stand as the sole guardian amidst a team primarily comprised of scientists and civilians.

During a voyage to the remote Sector X234, a red beacon suddenly emerges on the radar, indicating the approach of an unidentified alien spaceship. All attempts to establish communication fail, and in an instant, a peculiar pulse emitted by the foreign vessel disables the functioning of HermesX, leaving it incapacitated.

A mysterious green gas permeates the interior of the spaceship, causing everyone to lose consciousness. You manage to resist the effects for a brief period, but eventually succumb to the overwhelming force, gradually fading into unconsciousness. Your last recollection consists of witnessing the main doors opening and hearing the heavy footsteps of an intruder traversing the ship...

HermesX is a gripping dark Sci-Fi FPS game that blends adventure and horror elements seamlessly. Traverse the various decks of your expansive spacecraft, now infested with corrupted crew members and alien creatures, as you uncover the sinister truth behind the invasion.

  • Embark on a journey through multiple immersive and eerie levels in virtual reality.
  • Engage in intense combat, wielding an extensive array of melee and ranged weaponry, against both friends and foes.
  • Utilize stealth and ingenuity to outmaneuver ambushes and evade treacherous traps.
  • Make critical choices that can determine the line between life and death.
