Serona

❮ ❯

In the year 2120, humanity stood at the pinnacle of multidimensional exploration. It was during this era that the support vessel known as SERONA embarked on a routine mission, destined to station above the enigmatic planet of Mars. The objective was clear: to gather and relay vital information to a dedicated research unit stationed on the red planet.

However, fate took an unexpected turn, shrouding the mission in uncertainty. Contact with the SERONA was abruptly severed, leaving no trace but the enigmatic camera footage captured by the ship's security chief.

    As the story unfolds, immerse yourself in the intrigue and unravel the mysteries surrounding the disappearance of the SERONA. Let us delve deeper into the key details of this ill-fated mission:

  • The Year 2120: A time when the boundaries of multidimensional research and space exploration were pushed to unprecedented heights.
  • The SERONA: A trusted support vessel, setting out on a mission of utmost importance above the enigmatic Mars.
  • Objective: To serve as a crucial link, providing vital information to the dedicated research unit stationed on the planet's surface.
  • Lost Contact: Unexpectedly, all communication channels were severed, casting a shroud of uncertainty upon the mission's fate.
  • The Security Chief's Camera: The solitary remnant, capturing glimpses into the unknown events that transpired aboard the vessel.
Prepare to embark on a journey that will captivate your senses, combining elements of suspense, exploration, and unexpected revelations. As you navigate through a complex tapestry of twists and turns, you will gradually uncover the truth concealed within the enigma presented by the security chief's camera footage.
