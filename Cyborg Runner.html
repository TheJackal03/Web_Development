VR Cyborg Runner : The Escape Lite

❮ ❯

Introducing VR Cyborg Runner: The Escape Lite, a demo version of a thrilling sci-fi endless runner for virtual reality.
Immerse yourself in the next step of VR evolution with this amazing game that takes you on a journey through wormholes, space jumps, and space warps while dodging spaceships, asteroids, and various other space obstacles. Experience the full Virtual Reality simulation with any compatible VR headset, such as Google Cardboard.

After the defeat of Morgul's invasion army, our Cyborg warrior finds himself at the spaceport, desperately seeking an escape. His only hope is to capture a fast spaceship! To his astonishment, the defeated Morgul Boss possesses a large fleet of spaceships, each with unique stats and abilities. Choose your spaceship wisely, as a difficult journey lies ahead!

Cyborg Runner: The Escape VR game revolutionizes the genre of VR endless runners with its incredible mixed reality gaming effects, high-poly textures, and HD graphics. Dive into the virtual reality world and conquer a series of challenging endless modes, each more difficult than the last. Earn in-game currency to enhance your spaceship's abilities or acquire a new one with different capabilities! Prepare yourself for the ultimate showdown against Morgul's forces and employ your exceptional guidance skills to overcome the impending dangers. Prove yourself as the legendary cyborg hero and triumph over the evil alien forces. Embark on breathtaking voyages and become the confederation's ultimate hero in no time!

    Game Features:
  • Compatible with all VR glasses.
  • Highly addictive gameplay packed with fun.
  • Realistic 3D graphics complemented by dynamic lighting effects.
  • Choose from a variety of spaceships, each with its own stats and abilities.
  • Enjoy different runner game modes.
  • Immerse yourself in top-quality sound effects with realistic sci-fi sounds.
  • Simple and addictive gameplay mechanics.
    Gameplay:

    Insert your device into the VR glasses and navigate by gazing. Control the spaceship's movements using the glasses; no extra buttons required.
