Who we are

Welcome to Helvete Games, an indie gaming company hailing from Athens, Greece and formerly known as TetraForge. We pride ourselves on creating authentic and enjoyable games that take indie gaming to the next level. While we have ventured into the immersive world of VR, our focus remains on crafting captivating mobile games, with an exciting future ahead as we expand into PC game development. With a passion for innovation and a commitment to delivering joy to gamers worldwide, Helvete Games is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of the indie gaming industry. Join us on this thrilling journey and experience gaming like never before.

HermesX

In the year 2322, aboard the HermesX spaceship, you are the sole military expert responsible for the crew's security. While exploring Sector X234, an alien spaceship approaches, leading to a mysterious encounter. Rendered powerless by a strange gas, you awaken to a dark and dangerous world filled with corrupted crew members and alien creatures. Prepare for a chilling sci-fi FPS adventure, armed with a variety of weapons. Survive intense levels, evade traps, and make life-or-death choices to uncover the truth behind the alien invasion. The fate of the HermesX and its crew rests in your hands as you fight for survival in space's depths.
